About Me
Hi! I'm Agustina a multimedia designer based in Buenos Aires

Experience

I am a versatile professional passionate about visual storytelling and with diverse experience in digital design: UX/UI, Web, branding, audiovisual.As a video editor I was able to expand my skills and delve into the complexities of video production, developing a strong sense of teamwork and project management.
I have recently embarked on a new adventure in visual design. I have made the decision to start self-taught in the world of graphic design and UX/UI design. With my experience in audiovisual media production and post-production and now my growing mastery of digital design, I am prepared to contribute to projects that require a combination of creativity, technical acumen and a global perspective.

Interests

My interests span a variety of creative and active pursuits. Photography allows me to capture and appreciate the beauty in everyday moments. I enjoy handcrafting decorative items, which lets me express my artistic side and create unique pieces. Roller skating is my favorite way to stay active and have fun, feeling the thrill of movement. Additionally, I have an absolute love for 00's movies; their nostalgic charm and iconic storytelling have a special place in my heart, reminding me of simpler times.
